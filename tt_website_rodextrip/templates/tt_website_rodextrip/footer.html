About RODEX TRIP

RODEXTRIP is a travel online reservation system owned by PT. Roda Express Sukses Mandiri, based in Indonesia, for its registered agent. Skytors provide some products such as airline, train, themes park tickets, and many more.

We build this application for our existing partner and public users who register themselves on our application. After registration, users need to wait for verification / approval by our Head Office. We build our application for approved users, so that's why public user can't use our application.

About RODEX TRIP

PT. Roda Express Sukses Mandiri is a company engaged in the field of Tour & Travel and is known as RODEX TOUR & TRAVEL. Rodex Tour & Travel was first established on September 9, 2009 at a leading shopping center in Surabaya, namely the Mall Pakuwon Trade Center (PTC). Under the leadership of Mr. Galuh Suryono Cipto Adi, Rodex Tour & Travel has experienced significant developments from year to year.

Until now, we have approximately 1,000 registered resellers and dozens of Mitra (Citra) branches spread throughout Indonesia.
